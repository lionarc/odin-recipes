Bkeila

Description

Bkeila is a traditional Tunisian dish made with spinach and spices. It is often served with bread or as a side dish. The rich flavors and aromatic spices make it a favorite in Tunisian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Harissa
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the spinach.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic.
  3. Add the spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Stir in harissa, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook for a few more minutes until the flavors are well combined.
