Cucumber Salad

Description

Cucumber salad is a refreshing and light salad that pairs perfectly as a side dish with many meals. The combination of crunchy cucumbers and a tangy dressing makes it a favorite in summer cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Oil
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumbers and slice them.
  2. Sprinkle with salt and let sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Drain the water and mix the cucumbers with vinegar, oil, and pepper.
